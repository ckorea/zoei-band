ZOEI emerged from late-night coffeehouse sessions and shared dreams of sonic transcendence. We're four kindred spirits who found each other through a love of complex rhythms, ethereal melodies, and the spaces where folk wisdom meets progressive rock.

Our sound draws from global traditions - tabla rhythms, Celtic melodies, Middle Eastern scales - woven together with modern production and a distinctly analog warmth. Think Radiohead meeting Joni Mitchell at a monastery, with our mascot panda providing moral support.

Every song is an invitation to zone out, tune in, and find your center in an increasingly chaotic world. We believe music is medicine, community is sacred, and there's magic in the margins.
